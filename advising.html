Advising Information

Introduction

Welcome! If you're here, you're likely interested in working with me as a student or researcher. Here's some information that might help you understand how I approach advising and what I'm looking for in potential advisees.

Research Interests

My research interests are primarily in the fields of Machine Learning, Autonomous Systems, and Artificial Intelligence. I am particularly focused on theoretical and applied aspects of these fields.

What I Look For

When considering students for advising, I look for the following qualities:

  • Strong foundational knowledge in relevant fields
  • Proactive and independent working style
  • Genuine interest and passion for the research topics
  • Good communication and collaboration skills

How to Reach Out

If you are interested in discussing a potential research opportunity, please reach out via email. Include the following in your email:

  • A brief introduction about yourself
  • Your current CV
  • A statement of your research interests and how they align with mine
  • Any relevant publications or projects you have worked on

My email: yg [at] robots.ox.ac.uk
